Data Visualization‎

- The Most Efficient Method to Understand and Present Your Data -

Why Data Visualization is Important?


Because of the way the human brain processes information, using charts or graphs to visualize large amounts of complex data is easier than poring over spreadsheets or reports. Data visualization is a quick, easy way to convey concepts in a universal manner – and you can experiment with different scenarios by making slight adjustments.


What Makes Good Data Visualization?
Everything depends on the concept of layering, establishing hierarchies and making them clear. Challenging the viewer to think about the substance rather than about the methodology, graphic design, or the technology used to make the visualization. Encourage the eye to compare, reveal the data at several levels of detail, from a broad overview to minute statistics.
